Keynote

 

Innovations in Analytics That You Can Implement Now: Collaborative BI, Machine Learning, and Voice-Driven Analytics

While MicroStrategy 10 was recently identified as a market leader in the Forrester Wave™ report for Enterprise BI, we’re not resting on our laurels. We’re continuing to innovate and improve the platform by bringing exciting new features to market. Join us to see the latest in enterprise analytics and mobility, including customer success stories, new education and service offerings, and exciting announcements about new capabilities and products.

The latest release of MicroStrategy 10 delivers a completely revamped data discovery experience; a new, consumer-friendly way to interact with analytics that business users will love; collaborative analytics; hierarchical financial reporting; new ways to customize, embed, and extend your analytics; and enhanced tools for leveraging big data. Version 10.9 also includes improvements in the new MicroStrategy for AWS offering. The keynote will feature live demonstrations of using MicroStrategy for machine learning and voice-driven analytics with Alexa.

Speaker

Michael J. Saylor

President, Chairman of the Board, and CEO, MicroStrategy

Mr. Saylor is a technologist, entrepreneur, business executive, philanthropist, and best-selling author. He currently serves as Chairman of the Board of Directors and Chief Executive Officer of MicroStrategy, Inc. Since co-founding the company at the age of 24, Mr. Saylor has built MicroStrategy into a global leader in business intelligence, mobile software, and cloud-based services. In 2012, he authored The Mobile Wave: How Mobile Intelligence Will Change Everything, which earned a spot on The New York Times Best Sellers list. Mr. Saylor attended the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, receiving an S.B. in Aeronautics and Astronautics and an S.B. in Science, Technology, and Society.
